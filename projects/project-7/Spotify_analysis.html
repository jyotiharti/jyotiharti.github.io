Spotify Streaming Trend Analysis

Description:

Spotify Wrapped has become a social and cultural phenomenon, showcasing listeners' most-streamed artists, tracks, total minutes listened, and personalized playlists. Using the provided dataset, I conducted an in-depth analysis, exploring every aspect of the data to uncover key insights into listening habits, trends, and user engagement.This analysis is performed using data modeling and visualization techniques in Power BI.

Objective:

The objective of this project is to analyze and visualize the Spotify user's complete music streaming history data, including timestamps, track, artist, and album names, and reasons for playing and ending each track.

The project seeks to address the following questions:

  • Which artist did they listen to the most this year? Is it the same as last year?
  • Which songs have they played the most? How often do they skip them?
  • What time of day do they typically listen to music?
  • How often do they explore new artists versus replaying favorites?

Source: https://mavenanalytics.io/challenges

Overview:

This project explores user listening behavior over the past year. It identifies their most-listened-to artist and compares it with the previous year's favorite. It highlights their top-played songs and examines how frequently they skip them. The analysis also reveals the times of day they most often listen to music. Lastly, it looks at their listening habits in terms of discovering new artists versus replaying familiar favorites, offering insights into their musical preferences and exploration patterns. The data uncovers whether listening patterns are consistent or evolving year over year. It also sheds light on engagement levels with favorite tracks versus newly discovered content.
