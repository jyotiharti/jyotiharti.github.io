Pixar Films in Data

Description:

Structured information about every Pixar film from Toy Story (1995) to Inside Out 2 (2024). It lists each film’s creators (storywriters, screenwriters, directors, composers, and producers), budget, box-office earnings, critic ratings, Oscar nominations and more. This analysis is performed using data modeling and visualization techniques in Power BI.

Objective:

The objective of this project is to analyze and visualize the success of Pixar movies using an interactive and data-driven dashboard. This dashboard provides insights into box office performance, reviews, awards, and crew contributions, allowing users to explore the financial and critical reception of Pixar films in an engaging and immersive experience.

Source: https://mavenanalytics.io/challenges
