2. Positive Points of Election 2024

  • High Voter Turnout: The election witnessed a record high voter turnout, reflecting the active participation and engagement of the electorate in the democratic process.
  • Peaceful Conduct: The election was largely peaceful with minimal reports of violence or disturbances, showcasing the maturity and stability of the electoral process.
  • Technological Advancements: The use of advanced technology in voting and counting processes ensured greater accuracy and efficiency in the election results.
  • Increased Representation: There was a noticeable increase in the representation of women and minority candidates, indicating a more inclusive political environment.

3. Negative Points of Election 2024

  • Electoral Disparities: Despite high voter turnout, there were reports of electoral disparities in certain regions, with some constituencies experiencing issues such as voter suppression or technical glitches.
  • Campaign Malpractices: Instances of campaign malpractices, including misinformation and the use of unethical tactics, were reported, raising concerns about the integrity of the electoral campaigns.
  • Polarization: The election highlighted deep political and social polarization, with divisive rhetoric dominating much of the campaign discourse.
  • Delayed Results: In some areas, delays in the counting process led to uncertainty and speculation, affecting the overall transparency and trust in the election outcomes.
